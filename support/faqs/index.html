Am I able to sell or redistribute the wallpapers I create, and if so will I need to add a copyright stating that they were built by this application?

You can do whatever you want with your wallpapers, sell them, give them away for free or whatever, its up to you, and no, you do not need to add a copyright notice of any kind.

I want to create a Live Wallpaper which your application currently does not support, what should I do?

We would love for you to get in touch with us so we can try and get your idea implemented into the application, for all to enjoy.

You can get in touch by simply filling out a form on the Contact Us page

The motion-sensored Live Wallpapers (shake, rotate, etc) are not working on my phone, what shall I do?

This happens when you do not have the accelerometer or the rotate screen featured enabled on your device.

Please refer to your device's manual for instructions on how to activate these options.
