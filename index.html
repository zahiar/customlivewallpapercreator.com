Easy to Use

CLWC is designed to be as user friendly as possible, therefore anyone regardless of their technical ability is able to create their very own Live Wallpaper with a few simple clicks.

Multiple Live Wallpapers

CLWC allows you to create a variety of different Live Wallpapers such as Shake, Rotate, Animated GIF, Matrix and many, many more.

Visually Stunning

CLWC displays a life-like preview of the Live Wallpaper you are currently building, so you know exactly how the Live Wallpaper may look like on your Android device.

Its Free!

Yes, you read it correctly. CLWC is free to use and all Live Wallpapers created do not contain any visible watermark (we hate it when other applications do it, that's why we don't), so feel free to do whatever you want with them.
